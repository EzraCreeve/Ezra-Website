Our Story

Built on Purpose,
Rooted in Faith.

Ezra Solutions is a Christian-founded digital services company. Our name carries meaning — Ezra means "Help" in Hebrew — and that is precisely what we offer: the right help, to the right clients, with the right heart.

Founded by Creeve Gevero, the company grew organically from helping a single church organize its operations into a full-service digital agency. Faith is not a background detail — it shapes how we communicate, how we deliver our work, and how we treat every client.
